Who we really are &
why choose us

Kinoro originates from the word "Kinorohingan" of Kadazun-Duzun language which means "Forest of Life". Due to uncontrollable deforestation, tree planting are highly encourages to maintain sustainbility for future generation. We are a group of young people who are passionate about the environment and want to make a difference. We are a non-profit organization that aims to plant trees in Malaysia. We are also a platform that provides information about the environment and how to take care of the environment. Help us to plant trees and make a difference.
